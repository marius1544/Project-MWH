# Privacy policy

This application collects only the data necessary to create and manage a user account. This may include a unique user ID, login credentials, and a record of user consent.

Personal data is used solely to provide the service and is not shared with third parties. Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data from unauthorized access.

Users have the right to delete their account and withdraw consent at any time. When an account is deleted, personal data is removed. Public contributions may remain in anonymized form.

By creating an account and checking the consent box, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described above.
